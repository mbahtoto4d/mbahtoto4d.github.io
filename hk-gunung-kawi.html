The History of Gunung Kawi

The history of Gunung Kawi dates back to the 11th century. It is believed to have been built by Anak Wungsu, the last king of the Medang Kingdom. In the 19th century, the complex was abandoned and fell into disrepair. In the early 20th century, the complex was restored and has since become one of the most popular tourist attractions in Bali.
